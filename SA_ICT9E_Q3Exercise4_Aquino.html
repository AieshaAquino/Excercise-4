Pico De Loro Sa Nasgbu Batangas

Around the midst of the school year, my family and I visited Pico De Loro. It was truly a wonderful experience; the beach is enormous and calm, and there are several pools. The hotel rooms are nice and peaceful, and the balcony has the greatest view. The resort itself is enormous, and there are several themes in each room based on your preferences.
